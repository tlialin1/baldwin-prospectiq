# Check Railway Build Logs

## Step 1: Click on Frontend Service
In Railway dashboard, click on **frontend**

## Step 2: Click Deployments Tab
```
[Deployments] [Logs] [Metrics] [Variables] [Settings]
```

## Step 3: Click Red X
Click on the **red ❌** next to the failed deployment

## Step 4: Read Error
Common errors:
- "Dockerfile not found"
- "Build failed"
- "Port not exposed"
- "Start command not found"

## What to Look For

The error message will tell us exactly what's wrong.
